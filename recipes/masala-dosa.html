Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • Idli rice, urad dal, chana dal: 500g
  • fenugreek seeds: 10g
  • Potatoes: 4 units
  • Vegetable oil: 2 tbsp
  • Onions: 100g
  • Chopped ginger, garlic: 20g
  • Curry leaves: 10 leaves
  • Green chillies: 2 units

Preparation

Prepare batter by finely grinding idly rice, urad dal, channa dal, fenugreek seeds with water by equivalent volume. Grind till batter froths with bubbles on the surface. Prepare masala by boiling the potatoes to a semi-solid texture, before mashing in a bowl. Fry remaining veggies in a wok while adding required herbs, oil and spices. Lastly add the mashed potatoes and stir to a complete mixture. Finish the dish by layering the insides the cooked dosa with the masala. Garnish the dosa with a dollop of butter or ghee, serve with a side of sambar and cocunut chutney.
