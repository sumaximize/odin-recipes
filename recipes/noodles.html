Chicken Noodles

Ingredients

  • Hand-made egg noodle: 250g
  • Chicken shreads: 150g
  • Onion: 1 unit
  • Capsicum: 100g
  • Spicy seasoning

Preparation

Cook rice noodles to al dente. Marinate chicken shreds in soy sauce for 30min. In a large saucepan or wok, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Cook chicken until no longer pink. Add garlic, ginger, and onions; cook until onions are tender. Add mushrooms and cucumbers; cook until mushrooms are tender. Add peppers, carrot, peanut sauce, oyster sauce, and hot chili pepper sauce. Heat through. Toss cooked rice noodles with saute. Garnish with sesame seeds.
