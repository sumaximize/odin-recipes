Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients

  • Pita Bread
  • Roasted Chicken Shreads: 250g
  • Garlic Mayo
  • Chopped Lettuce: 150g
  • Onion: 1

Preparation

Evenly toast both sides of the pita bread and spread the chicken shreads evenly on one side of it. Add the lettuce and other vegies while dressing the fillings with a tangy garlic mayo. Your tasty Middle-Eastern chicken wrap is done !
