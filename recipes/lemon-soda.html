Lemon Soda

Ingredients

  • Lemon or Lime: 2 units
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Soda: 1 bottle
  • Salt: 0.5 tbsp

Preparation

Squeeze a full lime(s) to extract approx. 5ml of juice in a glass. Add sugar or salt to the extract depending on your tastes. Finally, pour in the soda while stirring to release the bubbles formed. Garnish with leaves of kothimiri (cilantro)
